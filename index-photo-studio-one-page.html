MY DESIGN JOURNEY

Since i was a child i'm not really interested in drawing or anything related to art. But the year that I graduated from high school. I thought i would choose to study business in collage. At that time my dad just asked me: "Are you interested in design?". My answer was no. When I submitted the application for collages, I felt that I wanted to go a different way from my other classmates. Finally I follow my heart and I decided to study design as my major. That's why I ended up at Raffles. This years was my second years in Raffles and i know it was the right choose.

“Believe in yourself.”
